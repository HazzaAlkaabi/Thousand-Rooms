Thousand Rooms

Thousand Rooms

Pencil, Paper & Desk

Illustrated by Rekka Bellum
Written by Devine Lu Linvega

You wake up in a room, surrounded by white unbreakable walls. On a desk, lies a pencil and a sheet of blank paper.
Inside the desk, notes bearing your handwritting, you have no recollection.

I

Cat awakens.

Owl finds a piece of paper.

Bat finds a pencil.

Fox opens the desk.

II

Cat claws at walls.

Owl leaves a message in the desk.

Bat finds a second pen.

Fox dismantles the desk.

III

Cat destroys the pencil and the paper.

Owl finds notes in the desk

Bat fills the desk with pens.

Fox builds the desk inside-out.

IV

Cat enters the desk.

Owl tries to understand.

Bat sees no escape.

Fox has a visitor.

V

Cat comes out of the desk

Owl understands the room

Bat awakens.

Fox has a second visitor

VI

Cat destroys the desk.

Owl finds a scribble.

Bat finds a pen.

Fox has too many visitors.

VII
